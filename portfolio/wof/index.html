About Work on Fire Institute

"We need a vast, well-formed army of evangelists," says director of the Word on Fire Institute, Jared Zimmerer, "who encourage one another, strive after a radical relationship with Christ through the sacraments, and leave their doorstep every single day ready to proclaim Christ in the culture." This combination of community and action is essential to the future Word on Fire movement. The new Institute is the primary starting place for this need to be realized.
